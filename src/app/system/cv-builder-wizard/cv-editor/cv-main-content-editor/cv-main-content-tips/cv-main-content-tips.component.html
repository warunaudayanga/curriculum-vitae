Introduction
You can access this dialog anytime by clicking the bellow button on the bottom left corner of the editor.
Content Editor
This is a editor for add contents to your CV. Left side shows how your CV content looks like after generation. Right side is the editor interface.
Add a Sections
A section is a part of your CV content. It can be a paragraph, a list, a name-value list, etc. Below is an example of a paragraph section.
You can add a section by clicking this button on the bottom right corner of the editor.
Sections
<Section Type> Type of the section
<Tile> Title of the section (Click to edit)
<Text> Text of the section (Click to edit)
